# Escaping the Build Trap: How Effective Product Management Creates Real Value (by Melissa Perri)

To stay competitive in market -> adopt a culture of customer-centric practice that focus on **outcomes** not outputs

**product-led** company -> solve real customer problems + achieve business goal

# Part 1 : The Build Trap

Build Trap:

- measuring success by outputs rather than outcomes
- focusing on shipping and developing features rather than on the actual value those things produce
- losing sight of what is important: producing value for customers, hitting business goals, innovating against competitors

> solution: setting up an intentional and robust product management practices

- When you have too many priorities, you will end up peanut-buttering your strategy: so many strategic initiatives spread over very few people

- Don't tie your people's bonuses to shipping software instead of solving problems.

## The Value Exchange System

- misunderstanding value == ending up in the build trap
- measuring value with the outcomes you create for the business and customers rather than the number of things you produce
