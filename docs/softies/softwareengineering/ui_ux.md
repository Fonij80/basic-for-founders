# Seductive Interaction Design

- We do not write in order to be understood; we write in order to understand. (—C. S. Lewis)
  **The principles of human behavior: why people do the things they do, feel the things they feel, and make particular choices.**

## Progress Dynamic

- like steps and levels in game, karate belt colors (different colors instead of one last black belt)

### Why?

1. **Sequencing**: more likely to take action when complex tasks are broken down into smaller tasks

2. **Appropriate challenges**: interest in challenges, especially ones that strike a balance between being overwhelming and being boring

3. **Status**: constantly assess how interactions enhance/diminish our standing relative to others and our personal best

4. **Achievements**: more likely to engage in activities in which meaningful achievements are recognized
